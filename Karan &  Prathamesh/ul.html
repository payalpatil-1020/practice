Car Brand

  • Mercedes-Benz

  • Audi
    1. Audi Sport (high-performance models, e.g., RS series)

  • Tesla
    1. No official sub-brands, but different vehicle models like Model S, 3, X, Y
  • Chevrolet
    1. Corvette (sports car line)
    2. Camaro (muscle car line)
    3. GMC (trucks and SUVs, sister brand)
  • Nissan
    1. Infiniti (luxury vehicles)
    2. Datsun (revived budget brand in some markets)
  • Hyundai
    1. Genesis (luxury vehicles)
    2. Kia (sister brand under Hyundai Motor Group)
